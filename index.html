How it Works

PwnyUp hosts challenges, processes entry fees, verifies winners and pays out the same day.
All you gotta do is play!

Challenges

Each challenge is 24 hours, organized by ranked tier and region. Say you're a silver 3 rank, you can only enter challenges with that rank and up. This keeps competition fair and fun.

How to Win, Earning Points

Your goal during this time is to play as many solo ranked matches as you can. For each match you’ll receive points for the stats you earn.

For example, if you have 3 kills and 2 deaths during a match you'll receive 10 points.

Verifying Points

At the end of the challenge all your match points will be added up and the top performing players will split the pot.

We get your league stats from the source, which means our point system and challenges are accurate, fair and up to date!

Challenge Entry

For now bitcoin is the only method of entry we accept. If you're new to bitcoin don't worry we'll help you setup up a bitcoin wallet.

Your bitcoin wallet is used to pay challenge entry fees and receive challenge winnings. You can cash-out at anytime with services to exchange bitcoin for your local currency.
