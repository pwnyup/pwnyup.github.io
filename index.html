How it Works

PwnyUp hosts contest, processes entry fees, verifies winners and pays out the same day.
All you gotta do is play!

Contests

Each contest is 24 hours, organized by ranked tier and region. Say you're a silver tier-division 3 rank, you can only enter contests that rank and above. This keeps competition fair and fun.

How to Win, Earning Points

Your goal during this time is to play as many solo ranked matches as you can. For each match you’ll receive points for the stats you earn.

For example, if you have 3 kills and 2 deaths during a match you'll receive ## points.

Verifying Points

At the end of the contest all your match points will be added up and the top performing players will split the pot.

We get your stats from Riot - just like lolking.net, op.gg, and lolskill.net do. This means our point system and contest are accurate, fair and up to date!

Payments

For now bitcoin is the only method of payment we accept. If you're new to bitcoin don't worry we'll help you setup up a bitcoin wallet.

Your bitcoin wallet is used to pay contest entry fees and receive contest winnings. You can cash-out at anytime with services to exchange bitcoin for your local currency.
